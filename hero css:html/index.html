Luke Skywalker

Early life of Luke SkyWalker

In Skywalker's early life, he worked on a moisture farm with his aunt and uncle. One day he bought 2 droids, named R2-D2 and C-3PO. R2-D2 showed him a message that was a call for help. It was asking for Obi-Won Kanobi. Luke set out and found Obi-Wan, but when he returned, his house was destroyed. He decided to let Obi-Wan teach him to become a Jedi.

To become a jedi.

Becoming a Jedi Isn't easy though. Luke had to learn many things about the force. He had two teachers that helped him a lot in becoming a Jedi. They were Yoda and Obi-Wan Kanobi. Though these teachers wern't able to keep Skywalker on dagobah when he went away to help his friends. He also learned that Leia was his sister!

One day, Luke goes inside the Death Star(The Empires giant weaapon) to disable the shields. Inside he has an epic battle. He battles his sworn enemy, Darth Vader, in an epic lightsaber battle.
